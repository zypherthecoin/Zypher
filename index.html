The Problem

U.S. healthcare payments are broken at the data layer.

The failure isn't just financial — it's structural. Legacy systems built on inaccurate provider directories and fragmented payment networks make every transaction slower, costlier, and less trustworthy than it should be.

$80B+
Uncompensated Care
Hospitals absorb billions in unrecovered costs annually due to billing errors, routing failures, and payment rails that weren't designed for healthcare's complexity.
~40%
Inaccurate Directories
Provider directory data across payers and health systems is frequently stale or incorrect, causing claim denials, misdirected payments, and patient harm.
Days
Settlement Delays
Traditional healthcare payment settlement takes days to weeks. Meanwhile, providers operate on thin margins waiting for reimbursement from insurers and CMS programs.

The Solution

Decentralized rails connected to real federal data.

Zypher's protocol connects on-chain payment infrastructure directly to authoritative federal data pipelines, making provider verification and payment routing trustless and programmable.

01
Verifiable Provider Identity
Provider credentials are anchored to federal data sources — NPPES, CMS, and HRSA — creating a canonical on-chain source of truth for provider identity.
NPPES · CMS · HRSA
02
Programmable Payment Rails
ZPH powers a payment layer built on Solana — enabling sub-second settlement, conditional payment logic, and composable billing primitives that legacy systems can't support.
Solana · <0.4s finality
03
zCARE — The Healthcare Stable Layer
Phase 2 introduces zCARE, a stable payment token purpose-built for healthcare transactions — designed to move value between payers, providers, and patients without volatility exposure.
Phase 2 · zCARE
04
Transparent Audit Trail
Every payment event is recorded on-chain. Payers, providers, and regulators get a shared, immutable ledger — eliminating disputes over what was billed, routed, and paid.
On-chain · Immutable
